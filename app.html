Poker Game Session Tracker

[We can edit this later] This app allows cash-game poker players to track their sessions using date, stake, buy-in, cash-out, and more. With this players can view models to visualize their win-rate (in big blinds per hour) and other statistics. Games can be edited/deleted after the fact, and there's no limit on the amount of games allowed per user!
