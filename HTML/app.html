Poker Game Session Tracker

Poker Game Session Tracker app allows cash-game poker players to track their sessions using date, stake, buy-in, cash-out, and more. With this players can view models to visualize their win-rate (in big blinds per hour). Sessions can be edited/deleted and there's no limit on the amount of sessions allowed per user!
